Background

FOUR PAWS strives for the improvement of animal welfare worldwide. They are a non-profit organisation receiving most of their income from donations and petitions with their other source of income, coming from other means.(acnc, 2014)

Research

According to the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits commission,FOUR PAWS Receives 99.95% of their gross income from donations and bequest (acnc, 2014), this is through their website and campaigns to help push and market their movement.

Observation

Observation of the FOUR PAWS social media accounts such as their instragram and facebook was used to better understand their target audience. The majority of the active followers on their social media are either current or previous pet owners between the ages 20 to 40.

User Profiles

User profiles were created to help identify possible users and their needs.

Ideation

Understanding our users needs - the common theme being more volunteering oppurtunities to be involved with the society as opposed to just donating. The website would have to advertise the donations, petitions and volunteering, equally. Research shows that 99% of FOUR PAWS generated income comes from donations and bequest whilst secondary research reveals participants would like to further their effort through volunteering oppurtunities.

In turn three strategies were used to ideate a website that could draw in potential users so that they could help join the cause.

  • Brainstorming.
  • Mood Boards.
  • Sketches.

Brainstorming

The brainstorm consist of ideas that will and identify objectives which will contribute to a possible solution to help engage users with the websites content and better understand their experience.

Mood Boards

For the mood board we took the idea of visuals from the brainstorm as it was a reoccuring theme that was brought up, words such as "cute animals", "videos" and "colours" became the focus. It's understood that visuals are what helps engage users with an interface and that was an experience we wanted to explore.

The inspiration for the moodboard was visuals and engagement. The websites present each have a level of engagenment with the site itself apart from the buttons. This would draw the users in and become more engaged and want to understand the mission of FOUR PAWS and from their hopefully make a donation or express interest in volunteering.

Sketches

Sketching the intial template will aid with the direction of the wireframes later on. The sketches will layout where different elements of the webpage will go.

Designs

The designs were based on the ideation phase pulling the best aspects of each idea and forming them into a user friendly website which engages the audience.
